Privacy Policy

Glasswings · glass-wings.com

Last updated: 9 June 2026

This Privacy Statement applies to all individuals whose data may be handled, whether as data user or processor, by Glasswings Events Private Limited (operating the Glasswings app and the membership community "Glasswings Party Club", together "Glasswings", "GPC", "we", "us" or "our"), a company based in India.

It sets out how we collect, use, manage and protect any personal data that you provide to us, or which we collect, generate and/or compile about you from time to time in connection with the products and services to which you subscribe (collectively, "Data"). Glasswings Party Club is wholly owned by Glasswings Events Private Limited and its respective directors and shareholders, based in India.

Glasswings is a members-only community for adults. You must be at least 18 years old (or the age of majority in your region) to create an account or use the Service.

1. Protecting your privacy

1.1 We are committed to processing your Data in accordance with required standards. This includes protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your Data in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including, where applicable, India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

1.2 Where required by law, we will obtain your consent before using or providing your Data for the purposes set out in this Statement, and only then will we use your Data in the manner specified.

2. Purpose of data collection

2.1 We may collect, use and disclose a range of Data about you for the following purposes:

  • processing your application for membership, and administering your membership and any rewards, of Glasswings Party Club;
  • processing your application, registration or subscription for, and providing and administering, products, services, promotions, games or events organised by GPC, alone or together with our business partners;
  • accessing and managing your account in relation to products or services provided or facilitated by GPC and our business partners;
  • with your consent, for the direct marketing purposes described in section 6 below;
  • analysing and maintaining customer profiles, behaviour and usage patterns, and conducting research, surveys, identity and data verification, fraud detection and prevention, security checks and other compliance checks (including, where applicable, anti-money-laundering and "know your customer" checks), and generating aggregated, anonymous or derived data;
  • complying with applicable laws, rules, regulations, guidelines and licence requirements in or outside India, as required by government authorities, courts, law enforcement, or regulatory or investigation bodies;
  • developing and improving our products, services, systems, operations, infrastructure and business planning;
  • enforcing the contractual rights of GPC and/or its group entities;
  • any other purposes to which you consent from time to time, and purposes directly related to any of the above.

The products and services above, and any directly related to a function or activity of Glasswings Events Private Limited, are collectively referred to as the "Service(s)".

3. Your data

3.1 We may collect or compile Data about you such as, but not limited to:

  • personal particulars, including name, date of birth and age, gender, and other details you choose to provide;
  • contact details, including email address and (where provided) phone number;
  • profile and behavioural information, including your profile photo, interests, preferences and activity within the community;
  • content you create, including chat and direct messages, photos, stories, poll responses and other media or text you upload or send;
  • event activity, including tickets purchased, event check-ins, and guest, waitlist or VIP list entries;
  • payment details relating to your cards or other payment methods, processed by our payment provider (see section 7);
  • account credentials, including your username and password (stored only in hashed form), account identifiers and service details;
  • device and technical information, including browser type, operating system, IP address, connection data, device identifiers and configuration;
  • usage and location data describing how you use the Service;
  • identity-verification information for the purposes of the Service or directly related purposes;
  • transaction and service history;
  • any other Data reasonably necessary for the registration, provision, administration or operation of the Service, or required by applicable authorities or law.

3.2 Where possible, we will indicate during collection whether a specific item is mandatory. Failure to provide mandatory Data may prevent us from providing the relevant Service.

4. How we collect data

4.1 We collect Data in a number of ways, including:

  • directly from you — for example when you create an account or profile, complete an application, purchase a ticket, send messages, contact support, or otherwise use the Service;
  • indirectly from our group entities or business partners;
  • from other members or your representatives, with appropriate consent;
  • from publicly available sources;
  • from our own records of how you use the Service;
  • from your participation in surveys or promotions organised by us or on our behalf.

4.2 If you provide Data about other individuals, you should first obtain their consent to such use, disclosure and transfer, and let them know where to find this Statement and how to contact us.

4.3 Cookies and local storage. We use cookies and similar storage on your device to keep you signed in, maintain your session, and personalise your experience. Some are necessary for the Service to work. You can manage or delete optional cookies through your browser settings, though certain features may not work properly if you do.

5. Camera, photos and AR lenses

When you use the in-app camera, your device's camera feed is processed in your browser to show the live preview and any augmented-reality (AR) lens you select. AR lens rendering is provided by Snap Camera Kit, which runs on your device; the camera frames used to render a lens are processed locally and are not sent to us in real time. Only the photos or stories you choose to capture and post are uploaded to our storage and shared with other members as described in this Statement. Snap may process limited technical data to deliver its lens engine — see Snap's Privacy Policy.

6. Direct marketing

6.1 With your consent, we may use your Data (including your name, contact information, device information, usage and location data, transaction patterns and demographic data) to send you direct marketing communications about our products, services, events and promotions. These may be sent by email, SMS, instant message, in-app push notification, telephone, social media or other means.

6.2 With your consent, we may also share your Data with our group entities and selected marketing partners so they can send you communications about the categories below.

6.3 Marketing communications may relate to: events, parties and networking; memberships, rewards and loyalty programmes; lifestyle, music and entertainment; food, beverages and alcohol; travel and transportation; fashion, beauty and wellness; and related consumer products and services.

6.4 You may withdraw your consent to the use or disclosure of your Data for direct marketing at any time, at no cost, by writing to us at privacy@glass-wings.com. You may also resume receiving such communications by written request.

7. Payments

Payments for subscriptions or tickets are processed by our payment provider, Razorpay. Razorpay collects and processes your payment details directly; we do not receive or store your full card or banking information. We retain records of your transactions (such as amount, date and status) to manage your membership and tickets. See Razorpay's Privacy Policy.

8. Persons to whom we may disclose your data

8.1 We do not sell your personal Data. We may disclose it for the purposes in section 2 to:

  • other members — your profile and any content you post or send is visible to the members you share it with, consistent with the app's privacy and messaging rules;
  • service providers who help us operate the Service, including Supabase (database and storage hosting), Vercel (application hosting), Razorpay (payments), Resend (transactional email) and Snap Camera Kit (AR lens rendering), as well as providers of customer, technology, analytics, marketing and professional services;
  • our group entities and business partners, and their respective service providers;
  • marketing partners, with your consent, as described in section 6;
  • regulatory bodies or government authorities, upon request or as required by law;
  • parties to a business transfer, such as a merger, acquisition or sale of all or part of our assets or business, subject to this Statement;
  • any other persons to whom you consent from time to time.

8.2 We take reasonable steps to ensure service providers are bound by appropriate confidentiality and privacy obligations, and that they use your Data only to perform the services for which they are engaged, and not for their own purposes, except as stated in this Statement or with your consent where required.

9. Data retention

We keep your Data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service and meet legal, accounting or reporting obligations. When you delete content, it is removed from active systems; some information may remain in backups for a limited period before being overwritten. You can request deletion of your account as described below.

10. Security

We use technical and organisational measures to protect your Data, including encryption in transit, access controls and hashed passwords. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Your rights and choices

  • Access and correction: you can view and update most of your profile information directly in the app.
  • Deletion: you can request deletion of your account and associated personal Data by contacting us.
  • Withdraw consent: where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.
  • Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable data protection laws, including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

12. Children

The Service is intended only for people aged 18 and over (or the age of majority in their region). We do not knowingly collect personal Data from anyone under this age. If you believe a minor has provided us Data, please contact us and we will delete it.

13. International data transfers

Our service providers may store and process your Data in countries other than your own. Where this happens, we take steps intended to ensure your Data remains protected in accordance with this Statement.

14. Changes to this statement

We may update this Statement from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you within the Service. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect means you accept the updated Statement.
